Lasagna!

Description:

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made by layering flat pasta sheets with sauce, cheese, and other ingredients, then baking it.

Ingredients:

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Cheeses

Steps:

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
